About Us & Our Team

NEXUS VANGUARD LOGISTICS SA is a fully integrated logistics company, serious, professional, and fully capable of managing complex national and international transport operations. We operate in a structured, transparent manner and in strict compliance with contractual obligations.

Our services include, but are not limited to:
  • End-to-end logistics planning and coordination;
  • Management and engagement of certified transporters and brokers;
  • Documentation management and customs clearance support;
  • Operational coordination of loading, transit, and delivery (POD);
  • Continuous operational monitoring and truck tracking;
  • Management of operational risks, fines, customs duties, and border contingencies;
  • Ongoing communication with clients, authorities, and logistics partners.
